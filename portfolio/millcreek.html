Millcreek Shopping Center

Shawnee, KS

Type

Retail

Size

29,178 SF

Millcreek Shopping Center is a 29,178-square-foot freestanding retail property acquired as a value-add investment in the expanding Shawnee submarket of Kansas City. Built in 1988, the center offers ample parking with a ratio of 6.17 spaces per 1,000 square feet and features amenities such as a car charging station. Strategically positioned along Shawnee Mission Parkway, the center benefits from high visibility and proximity to major retailers like Planet Fitness, CVS, and Hy-Vee, making it an attractive location for tenants. To optimize returns, the investment strategy emphasizes proactive leasing efforts and meticulous expense management.
