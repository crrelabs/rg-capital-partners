The Opportunity
Investment Overview

Pine Island Plaza is a value-add acquisition of an 11,041 SF multi-tenant retail strip center in Broward County with significant embedded rent upside, a comprehensive capital improvement program, and a transformative 385-unit mixed-use development breaking ground directly behind the property.

Value-Add Through Stabilization

A $250K capital improvement program (new roof, parking lot, facade, landscaping) will transform curb appeal and justify market-rate rents.

Significant Rent Upside

Several tenants paying well below $25/SF market rate, including one at $6/SF. Marking to market on rollovers and converting to NNN drives meaningful NOI growth.

385-Unit Development Catalyst

Plantation City Center (Related Group / BH Group) approved directly behind the property, adding 385 households to the trade area.

Seller Financing, 4.5%, 30-Yr Am

$2.2M seller carry at 4.5% with 30-year amortization and 10-year balloon covers 65% of the purchase price. Builds ~$296K equity over the hold period with a 1.67x DSCR from Day 1.

Impact on Pine Island Plaza
◆ Density injection: 385 new households within walking distance creates immediate demand for local services (medical, salon, insurance, dining) matching our tenant mix.
◆ Rent compression: Increased foot traffic and population density will push market rents higher in the corridor, accelerating our mark-to-market strategy.
◆ Vacancy absorption: New residents need services. Two vacant units become easier to fill at higher rents as the development takes shape.
◆ Cap rate compression on exit: Institutional-quality mixed-use development raises the trade area profile, attracting sophisticated buyers at lower cap rates.
