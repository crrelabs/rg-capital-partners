The Opportunity

Why Pine Island Plaza?

Pine Island Plaza is a value-add acquisition of an 11,041 SF multi-tenant retail strip center in Broward County. The property has significant embedded rent upside, deferred maintenance that we will address through a comprehensive capital improvement program, and a transformative 512-unit mixed-use development breaking ground directly behind the property — creating a once-in-a-cycle demand catalyst for commercially zoned assets in the immediate area.

Value-Add Through Stabilization

A $342K capital improvement program — new roof, parking lot resurfacing, facade renovation, and landscape upgrades — will transform the property's curb appeal, attract higher-quality tenants, and justify market-rate rents.

Significant Rent Upside

Several tenants are paying well below the $24/SF market rate — including one at just $6/SF. Marking rents to market on lease rollovers and filling two vacant units drives meaningful NOI growth.

512-Unit Development Catalyst

Plantation City Center — a 512-unit, 25,000 SF retail mixed-use project by Related Group and BH Group — has been approved directly behind the property, adding hundreds of households to the trade area.

Seller Financing — 5.5% I/O

$2.2M seller carry at 5.5% interest-only covers 65% of the purchase price — locking in predictable debt service at $121K/year and maximizing cash-on-cash returns from Day 1.

Value-Add Strategy

Capital Improvement Program

A comprehensive $342,000 CapEx program — funded from the equity raise — will address deferred maintenance, dramatically improve curb appeal and visibility from Pine Island Road, and position the property to command market-rate rents as the surrounding area densifies.

New Roof

Full roof replacement to eliminate deferred maintenance, reduce insurance costs, and provide 20+ year protection for tenants and the structure.

Parking Lot Resurfacing

Complete resurfacing and restriping of the parking lot to improve first impressions, tenant satisfaction, and comply with ADA standards.

Landscaping & Tree Trimming

Significant landscaping overhaul and tree trimming to dramatically improve visibility from Pine Island Road — the property's primary traffic artery and source of drive-by exposure.

Paint & Stucco — Facade Renovation

Fresh paint and stucco work across the entire facade to modernize the building's appearance, improve tenant pride of place, and attract higher-caliber tenants at market rents.

What This Means for Pine Island Plaza

  • ◆ Density injection: 512 new households within walking distance creates immediate demand for local services — medical, salon, insurance, dining — exactly the tenant mix Pine Island Plaza already serves.
  • ◆ Rent compression: As foot traffic and population density increase, market rents for commercially zoned properties in the corridor will rise — accelerating our mark-to-market strategy.
  • ◆ Vacancy absorption: New residents need services. Our two vacant units become easier to fill at higher rents as the development takes shape.
  • ◆ Cap rate compression on exit: An institutional-quality mixed-use development raises the profile of the entire trade area, attracting more sophisticated buyers willing to pay lower cap rates on stabilized retail.
